PLUS Research Group


The Privacy, Learning, Usability, and Security (PLUS) Group is a research group in the School of Computer Science at Tel Aviv University led by Mahmood Sharif. We are broadly interested in computer security and privacy, and primarily study the intersections of computer security and privacy with machine learning (specifically, within the area of adversarial machine learning) and human factors.

If you’re interested in joining our group, please do reach out.
